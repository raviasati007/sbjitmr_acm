ABOUT INSTITUTE

S.B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management & Research (SBJITMR) is an initiative of Shri Shantilalji Badjate, who dedicated his life to the field of education. His dream of building world-class educational infrastructure led to the establishment of the Sir Shantilal Badjate Charitable Trust, Nagpur. The institute is a self-financed, Hindi linguistic minority institution, affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, as well as the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State. It is accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC. Since its inception,

SBJITMR has gained popularity among aspiring students and parents due to its quality technical education, state-of-the-art infrastructure, robust teaching-learning practices, holistic development, and strong employability prospects. The institute offers programs across various departments, including Basic and Applied Sciences, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication, Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), CSE (AI & ML), CSE (Data Science), and Management Studies. With a dedicated team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members, SBJITMR has been granted autonomous status by the University Grants Commission (UGC), New Delhi, and Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU) from the academic session 2020-21 onwards. Autonomy allows the institute greater flexibility in academic development, contributing to the upliftment of academic standards and excellence.

ABOUT Conference

The Conference aims to provide a platform for the exchange of the latest Research, Trends, and Innovations in Science, Technology, and Management among Academia, Industry Professionals, and Students. It emphasizes discussions on Cutting-edge Technologies and Methodologies addressing Global Challenges, fostering Innovative Solutions. By promoting Collaboration between Scientists, Technologists, and Management Professionals, the Conference seeks to bridge the gap between Research and Practical Application. This environment encourages Interdisciplinary Partnerships to drive Meaningful Advancements.

Guidelines for Chapter/Paper Submission

Guidelines for Chapter/Paper Submission

  • Author should submit his/her article through Microsoft CMT.
  • Manuscript must be original and should not have been published previously/elsewhere.
  • The manuscript should be submitted, and author’s details should be mentioned in the beginning. The author should not be identified anywhere else in the article. The author’s details should include full name, affiliation, e-mail address, etc.
  • Manuscript must be written in English using MS-Word (doc or docx format).
  • Manuscript should contain a minimum of 4 pages and a maximum of 6 pages.
  • A maximum of FOUR authors are allowed per paper.
  • The font should be "Times New Roman", with font size 10.
  • Be sure to include Index terms or Keywords.
  • The manuscript should be in a single-column format.
  • Plagiarism should be less than 15%.
